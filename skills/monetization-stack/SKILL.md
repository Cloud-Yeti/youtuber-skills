# Creator Monetization Stack Builder

Build a revenue stack for: **$ARGUMENTS**

## The Core Principle

**Leave AdSense out of your budgeting.** You have minimal control over it. Build revenue you control. (Colin & Samir)

**Build on platforms where you keep 90%+ of revenue** (Kajabi, Gumroad, own site) vs. splitting 30-50% with marketplaces (Udemy, Skillshare). (Roberto Blake)

## Revenue Tiers

### Tier 1: Platform Revenue (Low Control)
- YouTube AdSense ($1-30 RPM depending on niche)
- Shorts RPM ($0.01-0.06 — 10x+ less than long-form)
- Don't budget around this. It's a bonus.

### Tier 2: Brand Deals (Medium Control)
- Typically lion's share of creator income (Colin & Samir)
- Scales with audience size and engagement
- Niche matters: finance/tech/B2B = highest rates
- Requires consistent upload schedule and audience demographics data

### Tier 3: Products (High Control)
- Courses (Ali Abdaal: Part-Time YouTuber Academy, 73% completion rate, 3,000+ students)
- Digital products (templates, tools, skills repos)
- Physical products (merch with margin)
- Software (SaaS built for your audience)
- Consulting / coaching / workshops

### Tier 4: Ecosystem (Maximum Control)
- Newsletter (direct line, no algorithm)
- Community (membership, Discord, Circle)
- Events (speaking, workshops, retreats)
- Affiliate partnerships (recommend tools you actually use)

## Roberto Blake: One Tribe, One Theme, Five Topics

Your monetization strategy should follow this structure:
- **One Tribe**: Your core audience (who they are)
- **One Theme**: Your transformational promise
- **Five Topics**: Five angles serving different needs of the same audience

Each topic can have its own monetization path:
- Topic 1 → Course
- Topic 2 → Consulting
- Topic 3 → Affiliate
- Topic 4 → Sponsorship angle
- Topic 5 → Free (builds authority)

## Key Benchmarks

- **Squatty Potty**: $45M first-year sales from single 4-minute video, 20M+ views day one, zero ad spend (Derral Eves case study)
- **Ali Abdaal**: Millionaire since 2020, net worth $4M+, diversified across courses/YouTube/sponsorships/affiliate
- **Jake Crabtree**: 120M views + $15K in 28 days from 5-8 Shorts daily
- **88% of YouTube videos** never reach 1,000 views — most creators never reach monetization
- **Educational content** gets 80% of views 6+ months post-upload — patience pays

## The Revenue Ladder

For channels at different stages:

| Stage | Subs | Primary Revenue | Build Toward |
|-------|------|-----------------|-------------|
| Pre-monetization | <1K | Products/services (don't wait for YPP) | Email list |
| Early | 1K-10K | Affiliate + small sponsorships | First course |
| Growing | 10K-100K | Brand deals + courses | Community + consulting |
| Established | 100K+ | Diversified portfolio | Platform ownership |
