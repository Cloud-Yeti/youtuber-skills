# Shorts vs Long-Form Strategy

Analyze for: **$ARGUMENTS**

## The Core Trade-Off

| Factor | Shorts | Long-Form |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Role** | Discovery engine | Revenue + authority |
| **RPM** | $0.01-$0.06/1K views | $1-$30/1K views |
| **Audience** | Cold, browsing | Warm, subscribed |
| **Algorithm** | Fully decoupled (2025+) | Separate evaluation |
| **Retention target** | 70%+ (100%+ = replays = best signal) | 50%+ |
| **Evergreen value** | Low (feed-driven) | High (educational = 80% views 6+ months post-upload) |
| **Production** | Low effort, high volume | High effort, quality-focused |

**Key stat**: Creators posting BOTH formats grow subscribers **3x faster** (YouTube internal data).

## The Growth Funnel

```
Short → Attention → Long Video → Subscriber → Revenue
```

- Shorts grow the audience
- Long-form earns ad revenue
- Brand deals, courses, products do the heavy lifting
- One deep-dive video earns as much as 10 viral Shorts

## Shorts-Specific Rules

### Ranking Signals (Different from Long-Form)
- Swipe-through rate
- Loop rate (above 100% retention = replays = best possible signal)
- Shares
- First-second engagement

### SEO (Different from Long-Form)
- Rank on: audio, hashtags, hashtag-search combos
- NOT traditional titles and descriptions
- Trending audio gets 2.5x more views

### Retention Benchmarks
- 70%+: Healthy
- Below 50%: Structural problems
- Above 100%: Replays — the strongest signal

### Max Length
- Now 3 minutes (2025 extension)
- Collaboration feature: up to 5 co-authors

### Monetization Threshold
- 1,000 subscribers + 10 million Shorts views in 90 days
- OR: 4,000 watch hours from long-form (standard YPP)

## Decision Framework

### When to Use Shorts
- Reaching new audiences in a new niche
- Testing a topic before investing in a long-form video
- Repurposing best moments from long-form (extract the rewatch "bumps")
- Building subscriber count quickly
- Trending audio is relevant to your niche
- You have a tip that can be delivered in under 60 seconds

### When to Use Long-Form
- Topic requires depth (tutorials, courses, explainers)
- You want ad revenue (10x+ higher RPM)
- Building authority in your niche
- Creating evergreen content (80% of views come 6+ months later)
- You need watch hours for monetization threshold
- The video has a natural narrative arc

### When to Use Both (Most Cases)
- Release the long-form video
- Extract 3-6 Shorts from the best moments
- Shorts drive discovery to the long-form
- Long-form drives revenue and subscriber loyalty
